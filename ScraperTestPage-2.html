A 243

Ingredients:

  • 2 cl Vodka (Absolut)
  • 2 cl Licor 43
  • 2 cl Blue Curacao
  • 3 dl Soda water

Mixing instructions:

Pour the vodka, the licor 43 and the blue curacao in a glass and stir. Fill up with soda and some ice cubes.

Creator/contributor's comments:

The name of the drink is my apartment :)
